About Us

Goodbye hassle! Get help instantly!

HealthAPPy is a single, trusted web platform with a mission to streamline the process of finding the perfect consultation, near you, with one tap. Our motto - An initiative to make health happy.

During epidemic or infectious disease outbreaks, a lot of healthcare providers are converted to dedicated centres for infectious disease care. However, a number of patients for essential surgeries and treatments such as diabetic persons requiring insulin injections, people on dialysis, fractures, cardiac arrests etc. still need access to healthcare data. However, these people have a hard time finding a hospital that would treat them because they might be dedicated care centres. We propose a solution, HealthAPPy, that provides details of HCPs that are NOT dedicated care centres, so that patients can reach there faster and directly, and save time in the process.
